Choose Your Method

How do you want to use QuizGenius?

Desktop App Best

Download and run on your Windows or Mac computer. Most powerful option.

Download for Windows/Mac
1. Create free account
2. Download and run QuizGenius.exe
3. Enter your API key

Chromebook (with permission)

If your school allows Chrome extensions, this works great.

Install Userscript
1. Install Tampermonkey extension
2. Click the link above to install
3. Enter API key in the panel

Phone + Chromebook For School Chromebooks

Use your phone to solve the quiz, watch on your Chromebook.

How It Works
1. Go to quiz on your PHONE
2. Use QuizGenius on your phone
3. Or use remote session to control from phone
School Chromebook Restrictions

If your school blocks extensions and console access, your options are:

  • Use your personal phone to do the quiz
  • Ask your school admin to allow the Tampermonkey extension
  • Do the quiz at home on your personal computer
